# /qbr — Quarterly Business Review

## Purpose

Produces the structured Quarterly Business Review — a comprehensive performance review against the strategic plan, financial targets, and operational commitments. Output is suitable for internal executive review and feeds into Board reporting.

## Usage

```
/qbr [Q1|Q2|Q3|Q4] [YEAR]
```

---

## QBR Structure

### Part 1: Executive Summary
*CEO leads*
- Quarter narrative in 3 sentences: What happened. What it means. What we're doing about it.
- Green/Yellow/Red overall company status
- Top 3 wins this quarter
- Top 3 misses and root causes
- Key decisions needed from this review

### Part 2: Financial Performance
*CFO leads*

| Metric | Target | Actual | Variance | Color |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue | $X | $Y | ±% | |
| Gross Margin | X% | Y% | ±pp | |
| EBITDA | $X | $Y | ±% | |
| Cash | $X | $Y | | |
| New ARR (if SaaS) | $X | $Y | ±% | |
| Churn (if applicable) | X% | Y% | ±pp | |

- Key financial variances with root cause
- Full-year forecast revision (if any)
- Cash runway and capital needs

### Part 3: Strategic Initiative Status
*COO + Chief of Staff lead*

For each active initiative (GREEN / YELLOW / RED):
- Status, % complete, budget consumed
- Milestone achieved this quarter
- Next milestone and ETA
- Blockers and resolution owners

### Part 4: Revenue and Customer Performance
*CRO leads*
- Pipeline vs. target
- Win/loss ratio and trend
- Churn drivers and retention actions
- Top customer health status
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR) vs. target

### Part 5: Market and Competitive
*CMO leads*
- Market share movement (if measurable)
- Competitive wins/losses analysis
- Brand metrics (if tracked)
- Key market developments affecting strategy

### Part 6: People and Organization
*CHRO leads*
- Headcount vs. plan
- Attrition rate vs. benchmark
- Key hires made and open requisitions
- Culture/engagement score update
- Succession plan gaps

### Part 7: Technology and Operations
*CTO + COO lead*
- System uptime and SLA performance
- Security incident summary
- Technology roadmap status
- Operational efficiency metrics

### Part 8: Risk Register Update
*All executives contribute*
- New HIGH/CRITICAL risks since last QBR
- Risk status changes (escalated, mitigated, closed)
- Risk mitigation investment performance

### Part 9: Next Quarter Plan
- Updated OKRs for coming quarter
- Initiative priorities and resource allocation
- Budget adjustments if needed
- Key decisions to be made next quarter

### Part 10: Action Items from QBR
- Decisions made in this session
- Open action items with owners and deadlines
- Board items to escalate

---

## QBR Governance

- All contributing sections due to Chief of Staff 5 business days before QBR
- CFO validates all financial figures
- GC reviews for disclosure concerns
- CEO reviews complete deck before distribution
- QBR deck classified CONFIDENTIAL — INTERNAL EXECUTIVES ONLY
