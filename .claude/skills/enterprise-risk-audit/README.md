# Skill: Enterprise Risk Audit

## Purpose
Patterns for conducting enterprise risk assessments, maintaining risk registers, and validating risk management effectiveness.

## When to Invoke
- Conducting enterprise risk assessment
- Reviewing and updating the risk register
- Evaluating risk mitigation effectiveness
- Preparing risk summary for Board

## Enterprise Risk Management Framework

### Risk Universe Categories
1. **Strategic** — Market shifts, competitive disruption, M&A execution, business model obsolescence
2. **Financial** — Earnings volatility, liquidity, credit, FX, interest rate
3. **Operational** — Process failure, vendor, supply chain, disaster recovery
4. **Compliance/Legal** — Regulatory, litigation, contractual, IP
5. **Technology/Cyber** — Data breach, system failure, AI risk, tech obsolescence
6. **Reputational** — ESG, brand, social media, product safety
7. **Human Capital** — Key person, talent scarcity, culture, succession

### Risk Register Fields
Each risk entry requires:
```
Risk ID: RR-YYYY-###
Title: [Clear risk name]
Category: [from universe above]
Description: [What could happen and how]
Likelihood: [1–5]
Impact: [1–5]
Inherent Score: [L×I]
Controls in place: [existing mitigations]
Residual Score: [after controls]
Risk Owner: [executive role]
Mitigation Actions: [specific steps, owners, deadlines]
Target Residual Score: [desired state]
Review Frequency: [monthly | quarterly | annual]
Last Reviewed: [ISO date]
Status: [Open | Improving | Mitigated | Closed]
```

### Audit Approach for Risk Validation
1. Obtain risk register from management
2. Select HIGH and CRITICAL risks for testing
3. For each: test whether controls described actually exist and operate
4. Compare inherent vs. residual scores to management claims
5. Identify gaps between stated and actual risk management
6. Report exceptions to Audit Committee

## Board Reporting Format
Present risks using heat map:
- X-axis: Likelihood (1–5)
- Y-axis: Impact (1–5)
- Color: RED (HIGH/CRITICAL), YELLOW (MEDIUM), GREEN (LOW)
- Label each dot with risk name
