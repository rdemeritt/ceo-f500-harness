# Skill: Change Management

## Purpose
Patterns for managing organizational change — communications, adoption, resistance management, and success measurement.

## When to Invoke
- Rolling out a major organizational change
- Implementing a new system or process
- Restructuring or reorganization
- Post-merger integration

## Change Readiness Assessment

Before any major change, score readiness:

| Dimension | Score (1–5) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Leadership alignment | | Is exec team 100% aligned and visible? |
| Case for change clarity | | Do employees understand WHY? |
| Vision clarity | | Do employees understand what the future looks like? |
| Employee impact awareness | | Do people know how THIS affects THEM? |
| Feedback channels | | Are there real ways for employees to raise concerns? |
| Training/support | | Are people equipped to succeed in the new way? |
| Incentive alignment | | Are rewards aligned to desired behaviors? |

Score <3 on any dimension: do not launch until addressed.

## ADKAR Change Framework

Every individual must progress through:
- **Awareness** — Why is this change happening?
- **Desire** — Why should I support it?
- **Knowledge** — How do I do it the new way?
- **Ability** — Can I actually do it? (training + tools)
- **Reinforcement** — What keeps me from reverting?

Communication, training, and management behavior must address all 5.

## Change Communication Plan Template

```
CHANGE COMMUNICATION PLAN
Change: [Description]
Owner: [Executive]
Date: [ISO date]

AUDIENCES:
  [Audience 1]: [Size] | Impact: [High/Med/Low] | Sentiment: [Expected]
  [Audience 2]: [Size] | Impact: [High/Med/Low] | Sentiment: [Expected]

KEY MESSAGES:
  Why: [The problem/opportunity driving this change]
  What: [Specifically what is changing]
  When: [Timeline]
  How affects you: [Per audience — must be specific]
  Support available: [Training, helpdesk, manager guidance]

COMMUNICATION EVENTS:
  [Date]: [Event type] | [Audience] | [Owner] | [Message]
  [Date]: [Event type] | [Audience] | [Owner] | [Message]

RESISTANCE MANAGEMENT:
  Anticipated objections: [List]
  Response strategy: [Per objection]
  Escalation path: [Who handles unresolved resistance]

SUCCESS METRICS:
  Adoption rate: [Target %] by [Date]
  Training completion: [Target %] by [Date]
  Survey score: [Target] at 30/60/90 days
```

## Common Change Failures and Prevention

| Failure Mode | Prevention |
|---|---|
| CEO announces and disappears | CEO visibility throughout — not just at launch |
| "Why" not communicated | Lead with the case for change before the what |
| Middle managers not enabled | Train managers first; they are the primary change agents |
| Big bang launch | Pilot, learn, scale — not all at once for complex changes |
| No feedback mechanism | Create visible channel; respond to every piece of feedback |
| Success undefined | Define adoption metrics before launch |
