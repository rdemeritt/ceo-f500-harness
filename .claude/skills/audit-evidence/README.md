# Skill: Audit Evidence

## Purpose
Patterns for collecting, organizing, and validating audit evidence for internal audit, external audit, and regulatory review.

## When to Invoke
- Preparing audit evidence packages
- Responding to auditor requests
- Building the initiative close-out evidence file
- SOX control testing documentation

## Evidence Standards

Valid audit evidence must be:
- **Sufficient** — enough to support the conclusion
- **Appropriate** — relevant to what's being tested
- **Reliable** — from a credible source (system-generated > human-created)

Evidence reliability hierarchy (highest to lowest):
1. System-generated reports with audit trail
2. Third-party confirmations (bank statements, vendor invoices)
3. Physical inspection or observation
4. Management-created documents with corroborating data
5. Management representations alone (not sufficient without corroboration)

## Evidence Package Structure Per Initiative

```
AUDIT EVIDENCE PACKAGE
Initiative: [INI-YYYY-###]
Prepared by: Internal Audit
Date: [ISO date]

TAB 1: INITIATIVE GOVERNANCE
  - Initiative Brief (complete, signed)
  - OKR alignment documentation
  - Executive sponsor approval

TAB 2: FINANCIAL AUTHORIZATION
  - Budget request form (BRQ-YYYY-###)
  - CFO approval with date and signature
  - CEO co-sign if applicable
  - Board resolution if applicable
  - Actual spend report

TAB 3: LEGAL CLEARANCE
  - Legal Review Request (LRR-YYYY-###)
  - GC Legal Review Memo
  - Any applicable regulatory clearances

TAB 4: RISK MANAGEMENT
  - Risk assessment (complete with scores)
  - Mitigation plan and owners
  - Risk register update confirmation

TAB 5: EXECUTION EVIDENCE
  - Milestone completion confirmations
  - Deliverable sign-offs
  - Cross-functional handoff records

TAB 6: CONFLICT OF INTEREST
  - Conflict declarations or "none declared" confirmation
  - Recusal records if applicable

TAB 7: IA SIGN-OFF
  - IA validation checklist
  - IA exit state document
```

## Retention Requirements
Corporate records retention schedule (GC maintains master):
- Financial records: 7 years
- Audit workpapers: 7 years (SOX-related: indefinitely)
- Legal documents: per GC schedule (contracts: life + 7 years)
- HR records: employment period + 7 years
- Initiative records: 7 years from close
