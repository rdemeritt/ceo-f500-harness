# Skill: Procurement

## Purpose
Patterns for vendor selection, contract negotiation, and vendor management within corporate governance requirements.

## When to Invoke
- Evaluating vendors or suppliers
- Negotiating contracts
- Managing vendor relationships
- Conducting vendor risk assessments

## Procurement Authority Matrix

| Contract Value | Required Process | Approval |
|---|---|---|
| < $10,000 | 1 quote acceptable | BU Head |
| $10,000 – $49,999 | 2 competitive quotes | BU Head + COO awareness |
| $50,000 – $249,999 | 3 competitive bids | COO + CFO |
| $250,000 – $999,999 | RFP process + GC review | COO + CFO + GC |
| ≥ $1,000,000 | Full RFP + GC + CEO | CEO + CFO + GC |
| ≥ $10,000,000 | Full RFP + Board approval | Board |

## Vendor Risk Assessment

Before awarding contracts >$100K:
- Financial stability check (credit, D&B, public financials)
- Security assessment (SOC 2, ISO 27001, or equivalent)
- Business continuity plan review
- Sanctions screening (OFAC, EU, UN lists)
- Conflict of interest check (any relationship to company employees)

## Contract Key Provisions (Required)

- Scope of work (specific and measurable)
- Payment terms (net 30 standard; GC must approve longer)
- SLAs with remedies (not aspirational — enforceable)
- Termination for convenience (company must retain right)
- Data protection addendum (if vendor processes company or customer data)
- Insurance requirements (liability, cyber, workers' comp minimums)
- Non-disclosure (mutual or one-way as appropriate)
- IP ownership (company owns work product)
